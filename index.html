about me

From Paris, France. 🇫🇷
Based in Fukuoka, Japan. 🇯🇵

My name is Ashley and I am a web developer.
I am currently working as a web developer in a Japanese company that owns several e-commerce shops.

During my seven years in Japan, I mostly worked in the sectors of customer service (sales representative) and translation.

I decided it was time for a career change in 2021, therefore I began studying web programming. I've always been creative and an excellent problem solver with high communication skills, and I believe it's one of the many qualities required to thrive in IT. I am always trying to think outside the box and I am still learning new things, especially in web development and I really enjoy this new career change !
